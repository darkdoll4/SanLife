### WHAT YOU CAN DO IN THIS GAME:(more to be added on) ####
1. Make your own story as you play. (No walls of text lore. You decide)
2. Have a phone (you can text, apply for job, go online shopping, look at maps, more explained.. below)
3. Work a job (Doctors, pilots, farmer, interior decorator, any job. ALMOST any job)
4. Get paid (You can work a night shift or a day shift.)
5. Own a home (apartment, mansion, house, etc)
6. Keep yourself alive daily (eat food, sleep, and hygiene. - not aggressive as in minecraft though.)
7. Express yourself (Clothes, either from an online store or in person. Very cool stores with different aesthetics.)
8. Decorate your home
9. Have a reputation
10. Friend, Date, Marry, have a family with an NPC
11. Do optional sidequests or missions for certain NPC lore
12. Travel anywhere (Main maps- hollywood, cascades, or vacation maps - japan, and more)
13. Travel and LIVE anywhere you want
14. Live to old age
15. Have the option to start a new life as a distant relative of your old game(sister, kid,)
16. Go shopping (clothes, furniture, food)
